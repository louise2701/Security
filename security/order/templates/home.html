Welcome to the Security Awareness! Our Project: Innovating for a Safer World!

We are a team of engineering students working on an innovative project aimed at developing cutting-edge technologies for demining. Our goal is to create a prototype capable of detecting landmines and unexploded ordnance, contributing to safer environments in regions affected by these hidden dangers.

Through this project, we also aim to raise awareness about the technical and humanitarian challenges surrounding demining. Follow our journey as we combine technology, research, and innovation to make the world a safer place.
